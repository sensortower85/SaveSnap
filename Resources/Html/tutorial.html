How SaveSnap for Snapchat works:

  • When you receive a snap from the Snapchat, Launch SaveSnap for Snapchat.
  • After successful login you can see the list of the snaps you have received.
  • Tap the one (which has available to download status) you wish to view and wait until fetch is finished.
  • After download is complete, you can view the snap as many times as you want and share it directly to social networks or wave to the library.

Please note! If you open the message in the Snapchat first, you will not be able to save the content in SaveSnap for Snapchat.
